Interactive Excel Dashboard

Healthcare costs in the U.S continues to rise each year, placing finanical pressure on patients as well as providers. I believe understanding which patient charateristics contribute the most to high healthcare costs can help healthcare professionals design better prevention programs, reduce risks and improve budgeting decisions. This analaysis uses a de-indentified insurance dataset to identify cost drivers such as age, BMI category, smoking status, family size and sex. The goal is to determine which groups generate the highest average healthcare costs and why.
